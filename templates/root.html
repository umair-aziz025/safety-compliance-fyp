Project Overview

The Safety Compliance Detection System leverages computer vision and deep learning techniques to simultaneously identify if individuals are wearing all required safety gear (helmets, vests, and masks). A person is only considered safe when all PPE items are detected, enhancing workplace safety across industries like construction, manufacturing, and healthcare.

Computer Vision Deep Learning Flask YOLO Models PPE Detection

The Problem

Current safety compliance monitoring relies on manual observation, which is time-consuming, error-prone, and inconsistent. In dynamic environments with numerous workers, ensuring everyone follows safety protocols is challenging. Existing camera systems lack automated analysis and real-time detection capabilities.

Our Solution

Our system utilizes advanced computer vision models with YOLO object detection fine-tuned for PPE categories. It offers real-time detection, visual compliance indicators, and analytics to track safety trends. The solution is scalable, adaptable, and ensures consistent safety monitoring across various industries.

System Architecture

Our system consists of five interconnected modules working together to deliver comprehensive safety compliance monitoring

PPE Detection & Classification

Advanced YOLO-based models detect safety gear like helmets, masks, and vests with high accuracy in diverse environments.

Real-Time Tracking

Euclidean distance tracker maintains consistent tracking of individuals across video frames for accurate compliance monitoring.

Compliance Visualization

Intuitive visual indicators (green for compliant, red for non-compliant) provide immediate feedback on safety adherence.

Safety Analytics & Reporting

Comprehensive reports highlight trends in safety compliance to inform policy improvements and targeted training programs.

System Integration

Seamless integration with existing video infrastructure via a Flask-based web application with user-friendly interface.
